OUR SERVICES

FABRICATION SERVICE

PROTOTYPE MACHINING:


We have dedicated staff and special equipment to work on prototype projects. This allows our production schedule to proceed without interference while assisting our customers in achieving the quick turn around time desired for prototype projects. We have an on staff manufacture & design engineer who can assist in the process. By continually improving the manufacturing process, we can make things more efficient, thus, saving our customers money.

PRODUCTION MACHINING:
L&T, our production machining services include milling, turning, and CNC machining. Our production runs range depending on our customers' needs.


ENGINEERING ASSISTANCE

Additional services provided by L&T include engineering assistance to support customer requirements. Our engineering department uses data and specifications supplied by our customer to determine the most effective way to meet or exceed our customer's expectations. We ensure that design, cost, and manufacturability issues are all considered, and result in optimal designs at the least possible cost, with the quickest time to manufacture.


ASSEMBLY

Value added services provided by L&T include complete system assembly. We have a designated controlled area where all systems are assembled fully wired. A final test is performed on both hardware & software components to ensure that pieces are fully functional.



OUR PRODUCTS

Products made at L&T Precision Engineering Inc. include parts from semiconductor, medical, aerospace, and various other industries. We work on all types of metals, plastics, and exotic materials. Our CMM Mitutoyo Bright-A910 has a table measuring system that accommodates dimensions of varied parts.



OUR QUALITY

L&T's Quality Manual is the basis of the systems used by L&T. L&T meets the requirements of ISO 9001: 2015. L&T is continually improving and driving toward a successful partnership with both customers and vendors.
